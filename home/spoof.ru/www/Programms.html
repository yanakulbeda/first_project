Programs and levels

Classes at the school Spoof Englisch School pass through the program, which includes 6 levels and is fully consistent with European standards of language skills (Common European Framework of Reference, CEFR).

Select the level and click the link to view the full content of the program.

A1 Beginner
    SKILLS:
  • Reading and pronunciation rules.
  • Removing the "language barrier".
  • To be able to navigate in an unfamiliar city, free to use the language of the tourist.
  • Translation of simple sentences from Russian into English.
  • Ability to write short messages on common themes, using the initial grammar and vocabulary skills.
    • Vocabulary: 600 - 700 words.
A2 Elementary
    SKILLS:
  • Securing reading and pronunciation skills.
  • Securing the skills to listen and understand spoken English language in the form of short dialogues and small blocks of information.
  • Talk about the events of bygone days.
  • Discuss plans for the near future.
  • Understand the basic content of the conversation or text.
    • Vocabulary: 1000 - 1300 words.
B1 Pre-Intermediate
    SKILLS:
  • Speaking skills and verbal behavior in standard situations and communication situations that require improvisation.
  • Being able to hear the key words and phrases to capture a general sense, as well as details of what he heard when listening to interviews, monologues, telephone conversations and excerpts of news of different duration.
  • Understanding the core content of the English language without any assistance, as well as the basic idea of the speech / text on the specialty.
    • Vocabulary: 1400 - 1800 words.
B2 Intermediate
    SKILLS:
  • Skills of successful use of the English language in a variety of situations, both standard and non-standard, requiring improvisation.
  • Understanding the main points in common sense and the content of any complex literary text.
  • Speaking skills: keeping the discussion and exchange of information, including the large number of interlocutors.
    • Vocabulary: 2000 - 2500 words.
C1 Upper-Intermediate
    SKILLS:
  • Ability to express orally and in writing the content of the professional nature of the text, read or listen to a foreign language.
  • Ability to prepare and translate business letters and documents.
  • Ability to write instructions and manuals.
  • The ability to conduct highly technical discussions in their field.
    • Vocabulary: 3000 - 4000 words.
C2 Advanced
    SKILLS:
  • This level allows you to deepen their knowledge in grammar, as well as get an idea of the style.
  • Expanding the concept of cohesion of the text types, syntax, vocabulary increases.
  • The student develops the skills learned them at Upper-Intermediate level.
  • The ability to easily read complicated original literature, including artistic and socio-political.
    • Vocabulary: 4000 - 6000 words.
